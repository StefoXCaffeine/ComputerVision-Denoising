In this assignment, the goal was to compare image restoration techniques and restore corrupted images. Given a grayscale-level image I corrupted with noise N the tasks were:
1) Characterize the type of noise affecting the image (Gaussian, Salt and Pepper, Uniform)
2) Devise a sequence of image processing steps to denoise the image
3) Adapt the ICM(Iterated Conditional Models) to train a Markov Random Field to recover the denoised image
4) Compare the effectiveness of the solutions by estimating the MSE(Mean Squared Error) and PSNR(Peak Signal-to-Noise Ratio) between the uncorrupted original image and corrupted image and between denoised 
image and corrupted image
